Winter Challenges in Edgemont Village

Edgemont Village is an upscale neighbourhood in North Vancouver, situated at the very foot of Grouse Mountain. Its elevated position makes it one of the snowiest neighbourhoods in the Greater Vancouver area, with properties regularly receiving significantly more snowfall than areas closer to sea level.

The combination of higher elevation, steep driveways, and premium residential properties means that Edgemont Village homeowners and businesses require a level of winter maintenance that goes beyond standard service. Heavy, wet snow can accumulate rapidly, and the steep grades throughout the neighbourhood make ice an especially dangerous hazard.

  • Highest-elevation neighbourhood in North Vancouver
  • Significantly heavier snowfall than lower areas
  • Steep driveways and hillside access roads
  • Premium properties requiring careful, detailed service
