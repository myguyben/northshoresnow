Terrain Expertise

West Vancouver's Unique Winter Challenges

West Vancouver's elevated terrain and coastal proximity create distinct winter conditions that require specialized knowledge and equipment.

Steep Grade Management

West Vancouver's hillside properties feature some of the steepest residential grades in Metro Vancouver. Our crews use specialized equipment and techniques to safely clear snow from steep driveways, walkways, and parking areas without damaging surfaces.

Higher Elevation Snowfall

Properties in British Properties and Chartwell sit at significantly higher elevations than sea-level neighborhoods, meaning more frequent and heavier snowfall. We monitor elevation-specific weather data to dispatch crews proactively.

Freeze-Thaw Cycling

The proximity to the ocean creates rapid temperature fluctuations that lead to dangerous freeze-thaw cycles. Our proactive approach addresses these conditions with proactive de-icing before refreezing occurs.

Premium Surface Protection

West Vancouver properties often feature premium paving, heated driveways, and decorative stonework. Our crews are trained in surface-appropriate techniques and de-icing materials to protect your investment.
