## Winter cannot be a reason you are closed

For a commercial property, a snowfall is a business-continuity event. Customers who cannot
park go elsewhere, staff who cannot reach the door arrive late or not at all, and the revenue
lost to a closed morning does not come back. North Shore Snow is built to keep you operating:
dispatch is triggered by weather monitoring rather than your phone call, so crews are clearing
your lot, entrances and main routes before the business day begins. [Snow removal](/snow-removal)
and [de-icing](/de-icing) are coordinated as one plan, sized to your property from satellite
measurement so you pay for your actual serviceable area.

## Safety for everyone who sets foot on site

Every customer, employee and delivery driver who crosses your property is someone you owe a
reasonable duty of care. One untreated entrance is all it takes for a fall to become a claim.
Consistent [salting](/salting) and ice control, applied on a weather-driven schedule and
documented on every visit, turns that exposure into a managed risk. Our overview of
[slip-and-fall liability for commercial property in BC](/blog/slip-and-fall-liability-commercial-property-bc)
covers what actually protects a business when a claim is filed.

## Docks, ramps and the surfaces that move your business

The surfaces that matter most are often the ones a generic snow plow ignores. Loading docks,
service ramps, rear entrances and the lanes that feed them are mapped as priorities in your
site plan and cleared around your delivery windows. When the 6am freight arrives, the apron
is ready for it. Priority zones are agreed with you in advance, so the crew clears in the
order your operation actually needs — customer access first where footfall drives revenue,
service access first where deliveries do.

## Documented, so "we were there" is provable

Reliability you cannot prove is worth little when an owner, insurer or claimant asks for a
record. Every North Shore Snow visit is GPS-stamped with time-stamped photos, filed by date,
so your winter-maintenance history exists before anyone needs it.
