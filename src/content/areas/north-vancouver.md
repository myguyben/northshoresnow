## Your local North Vancouver snow crews

North Shore Snow is headquartered on Esplanade in Lower Lonsdale — when it snows in North
Vancouver, we're not commuting in from the Fraser Valley to get to you. Our crews live and
route here, and our dispatch map is built around the city's defining feature: **elevation**.

North Vancouver climbs from tidewater to mountainside in under eight kilometres. A
December system that drops rain on Lonsdale Quay can leave 15 cm on Upper Lynn Valley in
the same hour. We route accordingly: hillside properties are serviced by crews positioned
uphill before the snow line drops, while waterfront routes are handled on freeze-thaw
timing — because down here, the danger usually isn't the snowfall, it's the overnight
refreeze that turns wet pavement into black ice.

## What we handle in North Vancouver

Strata complexes from Norgate to Blueridge, retail frontage along the Lonsdale corridor,
office and medical buildings around Lions Gate Hospital, and industrial sites in the
harbourside flats. Every program combines [snow clearing](/snow-removal),
[plowing](/snow-plowing) and [proactive de-icing](/de-icing) — dispatched automatically on
weather triggers, documented with photos on every visit.

## Bylaw compliance, handled

The City and District of North Vancouver both require owners and occupiers to clear snow
and ice from sidewalks bordering their property by 10am the morning after a snowfall.
Our North Vancouver service maps include your bylaw frontage by default — sidewalks,
transit stops and corner cuts included — so compliance happens automatically at the same
time as your parking lot. More detail in our
[strata snow removal requirements guide](/blog/strata-snow-removal-requirements-bc).
