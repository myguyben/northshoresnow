About NorthShoreSnow

Locally owned and operated in North Vancouver, BC. We are dedicated to keeping commercial properties safe, accessible, and liability-free throughout the winter season.

Our Story

Locally Owned. Professionally Operated.

NorthShoreSnow was founded with a simple mission: to provide the most reliable, professional snow and ice management services on the North Shore. Based in North Vancouver, BC, we understand the unique winter challenges that our coastal mountain climate brings to commercial property owners.

From sudden overnight snowfalls to the dangerous freeze-thaw cycles that create black ice, our team is equipped and trained to handle every winter scenario. We combine local expertise with professional-grade equipment to deliver results that keep your tenants, customers, and visitors safe.

As a locally owned company, we take personal pride in every property we service. Our reputation is built on showing up when it matters most, no matter what time of day or night the weather demands action.
