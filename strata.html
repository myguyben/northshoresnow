Common Area Maintenance

Complete Coverage for Shared Spaces

Strata councils carry a significant responsibility for maintaining safe common areas during winter. NorthShoreSnow provides thorough snow removal and ice management for all shared spaces, ensuring every resident can move safely throughout the property.

Our teams clear and treat walkways, parking areas, building entrances, garbage and recycling access points, mail kiosks, and any other common areas specified in your strata's site plan. We work around resident schedules and minimize disruption while maximizing safety.

  • Walkways, pathways, and pedestrian corridors
  • Parking lots, visitor stalls, and underground ramps
  • Building entrances, lobbies, and stairways
  • Garbage, recycling, and mail access areas

Liability Protection

Reduce Your Council's Slip & Fall Risk

As a strata council, you carry direct liability for conditions on your property's common areas. Slip-and-fall claims during winter can result in significant legal costs, insurance premium increases, and damage to your council's reputation with residents.

NorthShoreSnow's documented, proactive approach provides the evidence trail you need to demonstrate due diligence. Every service visit is recorded with timestamps, photos, weather conditions, and a detailed log of work performed, giving your council a defensible record of winter maintenance.

  • Timestamped before-and-after photos of every visit
  • Detailed service logs with weather data
  • Fully insured operations with $5M liability coverage
  • Monthly summary reports for council records

Strata Compliance

Your Strata Compliance Checklist

NorthShoreSnow ensures your strata meets all winter maintenance obligations. Here is what our comprehensive service covers.

Compliance Documentation

Complete service records that satisfy BC Strata Property Act requirements and demonstrate your council's due diligence in maintaining safe common areas.

Insurance Coverage

Fully insured operations with comprehensive commercial general liability coverage. Certificates of insurance provided for your strata's records and insurance provider.

Seasonal Contracts

Predictable budgeting with seasonal contracts that cover the entire winter period. No surprise costs or per-visit billing uncertainty for your annual budget.

Proactive dispatch based on real-time weather data ensures your property is treated before conditions become hazardous, not after residents complain.

Site-Specific Plans

A custom winter operations plan developed for your property's unique layout, including priority zones, equipment selection, and de-icing material specifications.

Council Communication

Direct communication with your strata council, including service notifications, weather alerts, and monthly summary reports for AGM presentation.
