Our Materials

Premium De-Icing Products

We select the right de-icing material for each situation, balancing effectiveness, surface safety, and environmental responsibility.

Standard

Rock Salt (Sodium Chloride)

The industry standard for de-icing large commercial areas. Effective down to -12C, rock salt provides reliable ice melting for parking lots, roadways, and high-traffic zones at an excellent value.

Premium

Calcium Chloride Blend

A high-performance blend that works in colder temperatures down to -25C. Generates heat on contact for faster melting action and is gentler on concrete surfaces than traditional rock salt.

Eco-Friendly

Treated Sand & Grit

For environmentally sensitive areas near waterways and green spaces. Provides excellent traction on icy surfaces while minimizing chemical runoff and impact on surrounding vegetation.

Pre-Treatment

Liquid Brine Solution

Applied before storms arrive to prevent ice from bonding to pavement. This proactive approach reduces the amount of de-icing material needed and keeps surfaces safer from the start.

Why It Matters

The Importance of Professional De-Icing

Ice-related slip-and-fall incidents are among the most common and costly liability claims for commercial property owners. Professional de-icing is not just about convenience -- it is about protecting your tenants, employees, customers, and your bottom line.

  • Reduce slip-and-fall liability exposure
  • Maintain safe access for customers and staff
  • Documented service records for legal protection
  • Comply with municipal winter maintenance bylaws

Surface Protection
